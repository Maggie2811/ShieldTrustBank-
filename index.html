ABOUT STB Bank

STB Bank is a forward-thinking microfinance institution dedicated to providing affordable, accessible, and sustainable financial services to underserved individuals, small businesses, and entrepreneurs.

CORPORATE BANKING

At STB, we provide businesses of all sizes with the financial tools and support they need to succeed. Whether you're a startup looking for funding or a large enterprise managing complex cash flows, our comprehensive range of services is designed to help you thrive.

TREASURY

Our Treasury department team is at the heart of ensuring the bank's financial health, enabling us to support micro-enterprises and underserved communities.

PERSONAL AND BUSINESS BANKING

Our Personl & Business Banking team , are committed to providing affordable, accessible, and personalized banking solutions to both individuals and small businesses, supporting small businesses is at the heart of what we do.
