Personal Banking

At Shield Trust Microfinance Bank, we are committed to providing affordable, accessible, and personalized banking solutions to both individuals and small businesses. Whether you’re looking to manage your personal finances or grow your business, we offer a range of services designed to meet your unique needs.

Our Personal & Business Banking team provides superior levels of customer service to meet the banking needs of the following two customer groups:

  1. High Net Worth Individuals with a minimum equivalent annual income of £100,000 in any currency or with a net worth (including owned property, and/or investments) of the equivalent of £250,000 in any currency.
  2. Small and Medium Enterprises

We understand that managing your personal finances is key to achieving your goals and securing your future. Our personal banking services are designed to simplify your financial life, helping you save, invest, and borrow with ease and confidence.
